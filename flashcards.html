How to use this deck

Before class

Cram all categories once, then switch to due cards. Keep going until rules, aids, safety, knots, and weather feel boring.

Before the exam

Do due cards, then a 50-question practice test. Any miss goes back into flashcards or the weak-area test mode.

On the dock

Use these prompts as verbal checks with a crewmate: one person asks, the other answers without looking.
