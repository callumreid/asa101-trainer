Interactive sailing simulator

A learning simulator for skipper decisions. It is not a perfect boat model, but it forces the ASA 101 habits: know the wind, pick a point of sail, trim, tack, reef early, avoid traffic, and answer rule/safety questions while moving.

How the simulator scores you

  • Boat speed: best on reaches, slower close-hauled, slow on a run, stopped in the no-go zone.
  • Trim: match boom angle to point of sail. Over-trim stalls; under-trim luffs.
  • Reefing: when gusts build, reef before the penalty hits.
  • Rules: decision cards are part of the mission. Wrong answers cost score and explain the rule.
