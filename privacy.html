Privacy policy

The short version: your practice scores never leave your browser, we don't ask for any personal information, and we tell you plainly about ads and affiliate links.

What this site stores

Your score, streak, and accuracy are saved in your own browser using localStorage so your progress survives between visits. That data never leaves your device, is not transmitted to us or anyone else, and can be wiped at any time with the "Reset saved stats" button on the trainer page or by clearing your browser data.

There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no forms. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any other personal information.
