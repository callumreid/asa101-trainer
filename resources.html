Resources and sources

This site is an independent practice aid. The curriculum is mapped to public standards and authoritative boating references, not copied exam material.

Official ASA material

  • American Sailing ASA 101 Keelboat Sailing 1 - course overview, scope, study materials, and certification expectations.
  • ASA 101 Keelboat Sailing 1 Standards PDF - public standards used to build the checklist and topic coverage.
  • Sailing Made Easy - official ASA 101 textbook. This affiliate search link helps support the free site.

Navigation rules and seamanship

  • USCG Navigation Rules Amalgamated - primary reference for sailing-vessel rules, overtaking, stand-on/give-way action, narrow channels, and responsibilities between vessels.
  • USCG Navigation Rules FAQ - plain-language answers on lookout, collision risk, assumptions, and common rule questions.
  • US Sailing Basic Keelboat - another respected basic keelboat standard and useful cross-check for beginner outcomes.

Docking, anchoring, and recovery

  • BoatUS Foundation - Docking - spring lines, close-quarters control, and docking/undocking guidance.
  • BoatUS Foundation - Anchoring and Mooring - anchoring sequence, scope, mooring approach, and swing-room basics.
  • US Sailing - Quick-Stop Rescue - crew-overboard recovery priorities and quick-stop context.
  • BoatUS Foundation - Crew Overboard - first actions, keeping sight, and recovery considerations.

Safety, gear, and weather

  • U.S. Coast Guard - A Boater's Guide to the Federal Requirements for Recreational Boats - PFDs, required equipment, weather, float plans, and pre-departure guidance.
  • National Weather Service Marine Forecast FAQ - Small Craft Advisory definitions, regional thresholds, and marine forecast terminology.
  • USCG Boating Under the Influence - boating sober and federal BUI enforcement context.

Aids to navigation and knots

  • BoatUS Foundation - Aids to Navigation - clear explanations of red-right-returning, lateral marks, safe water marks, and regulatory marks.
  • BoatUS Foundation - Navigation Lights - beginner-friendly navigation light recognition.
  • eCFR 33 CFR Part 83 - current U.S. Inland Navigation Rules text, including sailing-vessel lights.
  • American Sailing - Essential Sailing Knots - knot purpose and beginner practice context.

What this site does not do

  • It does not reproduce official ASA exam questions.
  • It does not replace an instructor, on-water practice, local law, or local notices to mariners.
  • It does not certify anyone. Certification comes from your ASA school and instructor.
