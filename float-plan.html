Float plan builder

Before a practice sail, leave a clear plan with a reliable shore contact. Do not file this with the Coast Guard as a routine matter; give it to the person who will call for help if you are overdue.

Use the plan: send or print it before leaving, update your contact if plans change, and tell them when you are safely back.
